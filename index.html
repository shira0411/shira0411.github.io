You control the main character (Keromi), navigating through levels filled with different platforms and var-ious kinds of enemies. “You can do it, Keromi!” comes with a unique friend-ship system that lets you befriend certain NPCs, including bosses to progress the player’s “character devel-opment”, the path which Keromi takes is in your hands. The primary objective is to ultimately defeat the final boss to be able to go back to the real world.

From the top-left, I’ve made pixel animations of Keromi attacking, walking, idling, and using her umbrella weapon to fly.
